# FPV Background Presets

Drop AI-generated (or photographed) JPG/PNG files here with the **exact filenames below** and they'll appear as one-click presets in the OSD preview's background picker.

Suggested output: **1920×1080** (or any 16:9 resolution ≥ 1280×720). Smaller works but looks soft when scaled.

## skyscraper-dive.jpg

"Gorilla whooping" vibe — diving between tall buildings.

**Suggested prompt:**

> First-person view from an FPV racing drone diving between tall glass-and-steel skyscrapers in a downtown city, late afternoon golden hour, slight motion blur, wide-angle GoPro lens, photorealistic, cinematic, 16:9.

## waterfall.jpg

Cinematic nature shot.

**Suggested prompt:**

> Cinematic FPV drone shot gliding close to a massive forest waterfall, cascading water in motion, lush green ferns and moss-covered rocks, shafts of sunlight breaking through the tree canopy, soft mist haze, photorealistic film look, 16:9.

## bando.jpg

Classic FPV freestyle location — abandoned industrial building.

**Suggested prompt:**

> FPV drone interior shot of an abandoned warehouse "bando", broken concrete walls covered in graffiti tags, rusted metal support beams, dusty rays of sunlight through broken roof panels, high dynamic range, GoPro ultra-wide, photorealistic, 16:9.

## dusk-lowlight.jpg

Challenging lighting — good stress test for font readability.

**Suggested prompt:**

> FPV drone dusk flight low over a quiet suburban neighborhood, deep golden-blue sky with first stars appearing, dim street lights just turning on, long shadows, low-contrast scene, tricky lighting, photorealistic, 16:9.

## After dropping images here

Refresh the app. The preset picker in the OSD tab's sidebar (under "FPV background") will light up the corresponding options. Clicking one loads it through the same IndexedDB asset path as a manual upload, so it persists across reloads.

## Licensing note

These presets are not bundled with the repo because (a) they're user-generated per-deployment and (b) the repo maintainer wants to keep the license story clean. If you publish your own deployment with AI-generated images, make sure your chosen AI tool's terms permit redistribution.
